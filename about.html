About Horizons Event Center

HEC was originally opened in May 2024, and has hosted hundreds of events. Ranging from concerts to ComiCon, we have something for everyone! Our goal is to provide a safe and enjoyable experience for all our guests. We are committed to supporting our local community and providing a space for people to come together and celebrate.

Host Your Event With Us

We offer a variety of event spaces to accommodate your needs. Whether you're planning a small gathering or a large event, we have the perfect space for you. Our team is dedicated to making your event a success, and we are here to help you every step of the way.
